## ![](/assets/icons/spec-var.svg)UIFlowCell {#UIFlowCell}
{:.spec}

```typescript
typeof UICell
```
{:.declarationspec}
Represents a cell (see [`UICell`](./UICell)) that grows and shrinks along with its content, instead of taking up all available space.
