## ![](/assets/icons/spec-var.svg)UIOppositeRow {#UIOppositeRow}
{:.spec}

```typescript
typeof UIRow
```
{:.declarationspec}
Represents a row (see [`UIRow`](./UIRow)) with all components aligned to the right (or left for right-to-left cultures).
